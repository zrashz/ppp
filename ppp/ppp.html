DIABETES

Diabetes is a chronic(long-lasting)health condition that affects how your body turns food in to energy.
Your body breaks down most of the food you eat into sugar and releases it into bloodstream.

Signs and Symptoms of Diabetes

  1. Increased hunger
  2. Blurred eyesight
  3. Cuts and wounds take longer to hard

Prevent Diabetes

Keeping weight under control,exercising more,eating a healthy diet,and not smoking.
